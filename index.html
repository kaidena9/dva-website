01 — Approach

Founded and led by David Amaro, DVA works with homeowners, architects, and designers on homes that demand careful planning, steady execution, and a high level of finish. David and a small in-house team see each project from the first conversation through closeout — the same people on site, in the meetings, and answering the phone.

02 — Services

A focused team.
A narrow project list.

We take on a limited number of projects each year so the work stays hands-on, coordinated, and built to a high standard.

  • Custom Homes

    Ground-up homes built in coordination with the architect, from early planning through final detail.

  • Whole-Home Remodels

    Full-scale renovations that improve how a house works without losing what gives it character.

  • Kitchens & Baths

    Cabinetry, stone, fixtures, and finish work handled with close attention to fit, layout, and use.

  • Additions & Outdoor

    Additions, exterior spaces, and supporting structures designed to feel native to the home.

03 — Craft

The work shows in the finish.

Good construction is felt in the alignment, the weight of the door, the way light lands on a surface, and how materials meet over time. We pay attention to the parts that hold up after the photos are taken.

Stone & Surface

Millwork

Lighting & Plumbing
