01 — About

DVA Contracting builds homes and commercial spaces people actually live and work in, and finishes them to a standard you notice every day.

David Amaro started DVA to do construction a particular way: a small in-house team, a short list of projects, and the same hands on the work from the first walkthrough to the final punch list.

He works with homeowners, business owners, architects, and designers on projects that need careful planning and a steady crew. David stays involved throughout, on site, in meetings, and on the phone.
